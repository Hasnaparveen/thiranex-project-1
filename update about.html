My Story

Designing for Everyone

I am a Frontend Developer with over 5 years of experience dedicated to crafting digital products that are not only visually stunning but also accessible to people of all abilities. My work bridges the gap between creative visual designs and strict semantic structures.

I specialize in optimizing web apps for WCAG 2.1 compliance, managing complex keyboard focus architectures, and styling with modern design frameworks like standard CSS grids, responsive containers, and glassmorphic UI.

I believe that the web should be an inclusive space. By writing cleaner, semantic code, we improve the experiences for screen readers, keyboard-only navigators, and users on low-bandwidth networks alike.
